## 💡 Tips for Success

**For Photos:**
- Natural lighting works best
- Take more photos than you think you need
- Show the piece from multiple angles
- Capture the lighting effects clearly
- Include scale references where helpful

**For Story Cards:**
- Write from the heart — your authentic voice matters
- 2-8 paragraphs is the sweet spot
- Focus on the feeling and meaning, not just description
- Reference the ritual and healing if relevant
- Let the poetic tone shine through

**For Product Details:**
- Be specific about dimensions
- Note any fragile elements
- Mention power sources (battery, plug-in, etc.)
- Clarify what's included (e.g., "includes USB power adapter")
