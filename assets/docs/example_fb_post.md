SCARBOROUGH
…where herbs scent the air and color invites a stroll. 💛
TEASER FROM THE STORYBOOK:
There’s a lane tucked between memory and springtime-
a narrow cobblestone street where lavender leans from windowsills, bookshops smell faintly of citrus and old ink,
and where every doorway feels like an invitation.
Scarborough is the kind of place your feet wander before your mind does.
A market street for dreamers, an herb-laced corner of Elsewhere
for anyone who needs a moment of color and calm. -eh&g
🌿 Meet the Piece

A three-building handcrafted market lane featuring:
✨ layered brick & book cover façades
✨ warm amber shop lighting
✨ boutique flower market
✨ open storefront florals
✨️ 3D embellishments to all 2D
✨ café balcony with red-gingham table
✨ vintage scooter vignettes
✨ bookshop signage & tiny paper goods
✨ sculpted greenery + ivy trails
✨ roof inlay and architectural details
✨️ enhanced shop signage & alleyway
A tiny street where color meets comfort and every shop feels like a familiar friend.
