## Executive Summary

You're building more than a website — you're creating **a digital home for Elsewhere**. A place where visitors can step into your miniature worlds, feel the magic, read the stories, and bring home a haven of their own.

This proposal outlines two paths forward, each with different timelines and scope. Both deliver a professional, custom-built e-commerce website that saves you $200-300/year compared to Squarespace while giving you complete ownership and unlimited scalability, without paying $700/year to connect all the various tools needed to market and grow. 

**The choice is yours:** Rush to launch before Christmas, or take the comfortable path with a refined January launch.

## Timeline Options

### Option A: Rush Pre-Holiday Launch

  * **Launch Target:** December 16, 2025
  
    + Before Squarespace subscription expires
    + One week buffer before Christmas
    + Catch holiday shoppers

  * **Scope: MVP Feature Set**
  
    + Core website structure (single landing page)
    + 5-10 initial products fully showcased
    + Stripe checkout integration functional
    + Collection grid with basic filters
    + Mobile responsive design
    + Newsletter signup active
    + Social links and contact form

  * **Deferred to Phase 2 (After Holidays)**
  
    + Seasonal homepage theme rotation
    + Theatrical lighting effects
    + Enhanced product photography
    + Blog/newsletter platform integration
    + Advanced filter options
    + Lighting demo videos
    + Google Form → JSON automation

  * **Development Schedule**
  
    + Week of Dec 9-13: Intense build sprint
    + Dec 14-15: Testing and refinement
    + Dec 16: Launch

  * **Why Choose This Path**
  
    + Capture 2024 holiday sales
    + Start building email list immediately
    + Establish online presence while current shop customers are engaged
    + Avoid Squarespace renewal
    + Can enhance and expand in January

  * **Risks to Consider**
  
    + Compressed timeline = higher stress
    + May need placeholder text initially (you refine after launch)
    + Product photos may not be professionally shot yet
    + Less time for iteration and polish
    + Holiday coordination (people traveling, less available)
